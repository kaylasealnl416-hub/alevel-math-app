# Pearson Edexcel IAL 课程大纲汇总

本文档汇总了从 Pearson 官网获取的 Edexcel International A-Level (IAL) 六个学科的完整课程大纲。

---

## 一、数学 (Mathematics)

### 单元结构
- **Pure Mathematics (纯数学)**: P1, P2, P3, P4
- **Statistics (统计)**: S1, S2, S3
- **Mechanics (力学)**: M1, M2, M3
- **Decision Mathematics (决策数学)**: D1

### 考试安排
- AS阶段: P1, P2 为必考，M1/S1/D1 任选其一
- A2阶段: P1, P2, P3, P4 必考，其他模块选两个
- 考试时间: 1月、5-6月、10-11月

---

## 二、进阶数学 (Further Mathematics)

### 单元结构
- **Further Pure (进阶纯数)**: FP1, FP2, FP3
- **Further Mechanics (进阶力学)**: FM1, FM2, FM3
- **Further Statistics (进阶统计)**: FS1, FS2, FS3
- **Decision Mathematics**: D1

### 考试安排
- FP1 为必考
- 在 M1, S1, D1, M2, M3, S2, S3, FP2, FP3 中任选两个单元
- 基础数学中已选单元不能重复选择

---

## 三、经济学 (Economics)

### 单元结构 (4个单元)

#### Unit 1: Markets in action (市场运行) - AS
- The nature of economics
- Positive and normative economics
- Scarcity and choice
- Production possibility frontiers
- Specialisation and the role of money
- Free market, mixed and command economies
- Demand curve and supply curve
- Price elasticity of demand/supply
- Determination of market equilibrium
- Consumer and producer surplus
- Indirect taxes and subsidies
- Sources of market failure
- Positive and negative externalities
- Public goods
- Imperfect market information
- Government intervention
- Government failure

#### Unit 2: Macroeconomic performance and policy (宏观经济表现与政策) - AS
- Economic growth
- Inflation
- Employment and unemployment
- Balance of payments
- Aggregate demand (C, I, G, X-M)
- Aggregate supply (SRAS, LRAS)
- National income
- Injections and withdrawals
- The multiplier
- Causes and costs of growth
- Macroeconomic objectives and conflicts
- Macroeconomic supply-side policies
- Macroeconomic demand-side policies

#### Unit 3: Business behaviour (商业行为) - A2
- Economic growth
- Business types and size
- Revenue, costs and profit
- Market structure (competition, monopoly, oligopoly)
- Labour market
- Government intervention and competition policy

#### Unit 4: Developments in the global economy (全球经济背景下的发展) - A2
- Globalisation
- International trade
- Balance of payments
- Exchange rates
- Economic development
- Inequality and poverty
- Role of government in macroeconomy

### 考试结构
- Unit 1 & 2 (AS): 1小时45分钟，80分
  - Section A: 6道选择题 (6分)
  - Section B: 5道简答题 (20分)
  - Section C: 数据分析题 (34分)
  - Section D: Essay (20分，二选一)

- Unit 3 & 4 (A2): 2小时，80分
  - Section A: 6道选择题 (6分)
  - Section C: 数据分析题 (34分)
  - Section D: Essay (40分，两题选两题)

## 四、历史 (History)

### 单元结构 (4个单元)

#### Unit 1: Breadth Study - Themes in modern international history (1789-1945)
- French Revolution (1789-1815)
- Industrial Revolution
- World War I (1914-1918)
- Russian Revolution (1917)
- World War II (1939-1945)

#### Unit 2: Depth Study - The USA 1918-1968
- Jazz Age and Roaring Twenties
- Great Depression (1929-1939)
- New Deal
- World War II and post-war America
- Civil Rights Movement
- Cold War context

#### Unit 3: Thematic Study with Sources - The British Empire c. 1855-1967
- Expansion of Empire
- Colonial rule and resistance
- Decolonisation
- Impact on Britain and colonies

#### Unit 4: Historical Investigation
- Student-chosen topic
- Independent research
- Source analysis

## 五、政治 (Politics)

### 单元结构 (4个单元)

#### Unit 1: UK Politics & Core Political Ideas (AS)
- UK democracy and elections
- Electoral systems (FPTP, AMS, STV, AV)
- Voting behaviour
- Pressure groups
- Core Political Ideas:
  - Liberalism
  - Socialism
  - Conservatism

#### Unit 2: UK Government (AS)
- UK Constitution
- Parliament (House of Commons, House of Lords)
- Prime Minister and Cabinet
- Devolution

#### Unit 3: Comparative Politics - The USA (A2)
- US Federalism
- US Constitution
- Congress
- Presidency
- Supreme Court
- Civil rights

#### Unit 4: Global Politics (A2)
- Globalisation
- Regional organisations (EU, UN, NATO)
- Human rights
- International relations theory

## 六、心理学 (Psychology)

### 单元结构 (4个单元)

#### Unit 1: Social Psychology, Cognitive Psychology, Research Methods (AS)
**Social Psychology:**
- Conformity (normative, informational)
- Obedience (Milgram experiment)
- Social identity theory
- Attitudes and attitude change
- Prejudice and discrimination

**Cognitive Psychology:**
- Memory models (multi-store, levels of processing)
- Forgetting (decay, interference, retrieval failure)
- Perceptual illusions
- Schemas and top-down processing

**Research Methods:**
- Hypotheses (null and alternative)
- Experimental designs (lab, field, natural)
- Variables (IV, DV, control, confounding)
- Qualitative vs Quantitative methods
- Ethics in psychological research
- Statistical tests (correlation, t-test, chi-square)

#### Unit 2: Biological Psychology, Learning Theories, Developmental Psychology (AS)
**Biological Psychology:**
- Neural communication
- Endocrine system
- Brain structure and function
- Genetics and behaviour

**Learning Theories:**
- Classical conditioning
- Operant conditioning
- Social learning theory
- Habituation

**Developmental Psychology:**
- Piaget's stages of development
- Vygotsky's sociocultural theory
- Attachment theory
- Development of language

#### Unit 3: Applications of Psychology (A2)
- Clinical Psychology (definitions, diagnosis, treatments)
- Criminal Psychology
- Health Psychology
- Sport Psychology

#### Unit 4: Debates in Psychology & Advanced Research Methods (A2)
- Nature vs Nurture debate
- Free will vs Determinism
- Reductionism vs Holism
- Ethical debates
- Advanced statistical methods
