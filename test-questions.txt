# A-Level Mathematics Test Questions

## Question 1
Solve for x: 2x + 5 = 13

A. x = 3
B. x = 4
C. x = 5
D. x = 6

Answer: B
Explanation: 2x = 13 - 5 = 8, so x = 4

## Question 2
What is the derivative of f(x) = x^2 + 3x?

A. 2x + 3
B. x + 3
C. 2x
D. x^2

Answer: A
Explanation: Using the power rule, d/dx(x^2) = 2x and d/dx(3x) = 3, so f'(x) = 2x + 3

## Question 3
Calculate the area of a circle with radius 5 cm.

A. 25π cm²
B. 10π cm²
C. 50π cm²
D. 5π cm²

Answer: A
Explanation: Area = πr² = π(5)² = 25π cm²
